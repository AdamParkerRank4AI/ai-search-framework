## ROUND 1: PREMIER LEAGUE RECORDS & STATS
*6 questions - 1 point each unless stated*

**Q1.** Who scored the fastest hat-trick in Premier League history? Bonus point for the time.
> **Answer:** Sadio Mane - 2 minutes 56 seconds (Southampton vs Aston Villa, 16 May 2015)

**Q2.** Which player has scored the most own goals in Premier League history?
> **Answer:** Richard Dunne (10 own goals)

**Q3.** Who holds the record for the most Premier League yellow cards?
> **Answer:** Gareth Barry (123 yellow cards)

**Q4.** Who holds the record for the most Premier League assists of all time?
> **Answer:** Ryan Giggs (162 assists)

**Q5.** Which club conceded the fewest goals in a single 38-game Premier League season, and how many did they concede? (1 point each)
> **Answer:** Chelsea - 15 goals (2004-05, under Jose Mourinho)

**Q6.** Who has scored the most Premier League hat-tricks?
> **Answer:** Sergio Aguero (12 hat-tricks, all for Manchester City). Alan Shearer is second with 11.

## ROUND 2: WORLD FOOTBALL
*7 questions - variable points*

**Q7.** Since 2002, six different countries have won the World Cup. Name them all. (1 point each, 6 available)
> **Answer:** Brazil (2002), Italy (2006), Spain (2010), Germany (2014), France (2018), Argentina (2022)

**Q8.** In the 2014 World Cup semi-final, Germany beat Brazil 7-1. Five different German players scored. Name any three of them. (1 point each, 3 available)
> **Answer:** Thomas Muller, Miroslav Klose, Toni Kroos (x2), Sami Khedira, Andre Schurrle (x2)

**Q9.** In the famous 2005 Champions League final, Liverpool came back from 3-0 down against AC Milan. Name all three Liverpool goalscorers. (1 point each)
> **Answer:** Steven Gerrard, Vladimir Smicer, Xabi Alonso

**Q10.** Which manager has won the Champions League / European Cup the most times?
> **Answer:** Carlo Ancelotti (5 times) - AC Milan 2003 & 2007, Real Madrid 2014, 2022 & 2024

**Q11.** Which country has appeared in the most World Cup finals without ever winning the tournament?
> **Answer:** Netherlands (3 finals — 1974, 1978, 2010 — lost all three)

**Q12.** In the 2010 World Cup Final between Spain and the Netherlands, which Dutch player was sent off?
> **Answer:** John Heitinga (second yellow card in extra time)

**Q13.** In the 2019 Champions League Final: (1 point each)
1. Who scored the first goal?
2. Who scored the second goal?
> **Answer:**
> 1. Mohamed Salah (penalty in the 2nd minute)
> 2. Divock Origi (late goal to seal a 2-0 win for Liverpool vs Tottenham)

## ROUND 3: WHO AM I?
*3 questions - 1 point each*

**Q14.** I'm South African. I scored twice in a Champions League final before I came to England. In the Premier League, I played for Blackburn Rovers and West Ham. I scored 18 league goals in a single season at Blackburn. I later became a first-team coach at Manchester United. Who am I?
> **Answer:** Benni McCarthy (scored twice in Porto's 3-0 CL final win over Monaco, 2004)

**Q15.** I won the European Golden Shoe while playing for Sunderland in 1999-2000, scoring 30 league goals that season. I went on to play for several other English clubs including Southampton, Aston Villa, and West Brom. Who am I?
> **Answer:** Kevin Phillips

**Q16.** I'm Irish. I scored over 125 Premier League goals across five different clubs, including Leeds, Liverpool, and Tottenham — where I spent most of my career. I also became a cult hero in the United States. Who am I?

## ROUND 5: ODD ONE OUT
*6 questions - 2 points each (1 for the odd one out, 1 for the reason)*

**Q24.** Odd one out: Manchester United, Arsenal, Chelsea, Manchester City, Liverpool, Leicester City?
> **Answer:** **Leicester City** - the only Premier League champion from outside the traditional "Big Six." (Also accept: the only one based in the East Midlands)

**Q25.** Odd one out: Steven Gerrard, Frank Lampard, Paul Scholes, David Beckham?
> **Answer:** **Steven Gerrard** - the only one who never won the Premier League.

**Q26.** Odd one out: Paris Saint-Germain, Real Madrid, Barcelona, Bayern Munich, Manchester City?
> **Answer:** **Manchester City** - the only club to have been relegated to the third tier of their domestic league. (City were in the old Division Two in 1998-99)

**Q27.** Odd one out: Didier Drogba, Thierry Henry, Sergio Aguero, Robin van Persie, Luis Suarez?
> **Answer:** **Luis Suarez** - the only one who never won the Premier League.

**Q28.** Odd one out: Petr Cech, David Seaman, Edwin van der Sar, Peter Schmeichel?
> **Answer:** **David Seaman** - the only one who never won the Champions League.

**Q29.** Odd one out: Ryan Giggs, Paul Scholes, Gary Neville, David Beckham?
> **Answer:** **David Beckham** - the only member of the "Class of '92" who did not finish his career at Manchester United. Giggs, Scholes, and Neville all retired at Man Utd.

## ROUND 7: QUICK FIRE TRIVIA
*13 questions - 1 point each unless stated*

**Q30.** What was the original name of the Premier League when it launched in 1992?
> **Answer:** The FA Premier League (also accept "FA Carling Premiership" as the first sponsored name)

**Q31.** Who is the youngest player to score a Premier League goal?
> **Answer:** James Vaughan (Everton, aged 16 years and 271 days, vs Crystal Palace in April 2005)

**Q32.** Which country did manager Claudio Ranieri manage immediately BEFORE taking charge of Leicester City's title-winning season?
> **Answer:** Greece (he managed the Greek national team)

**Q33.** Which two teams contested the first-ever Premier League match on 15 August 1992?
> **Answer:** Sheffield United vs Manchester United (Brian Deane scored the first-ever PL goal)

**Q34.** Name all 5 clubs that Sol Campbell played for. (1 point each, 5 available)
> **Answer:** Tottenham Hotspur, Arsenal, Portsmouth, Newcastle United, Notts County

**Q35.** Leicester City's 2015-16 Premier League title was mathematically confirmed when Tottenham failed to beat which club?
> **Answer:** Chelsea (2-2 draw at Stamford Bridge, 2 May 2016)

**Q36.** In which city was the 2012 Champions League Final held, where Chelsea famously beat Bayern Munich on penalties?
> **Answer:** Munich — the final was played at Bayern's own Allianz Arena

**Q37.** Which player scored the most Premier League goals in a single calendar year (January to December)?
> **Answer:** Harry Kane - 39 goals in 2017

**Q38.** Which player has won the most Premier League winner's medals?
> **Answer:** Ryan Giggs (13 Premier League titles, all with Manchester United)

**Q39.** Which club did Jamie Vardy play for immediately before joining Leicester City?
> **Answer:** Fleetwood Town

**Q40.** When Leicester won the Premier League in 2016: (1 point each)
1. Who was the Premier League top scorer that season?
2. Which team finished second?
> **Answer:**
> 1. Harry Kane (25 goals)
> 2. Arsenal

**Q41.** Which club did Didier Drogba join Chelsea from?
> **Answer:** Marseille (signed in 2004 for £24 million)

**Q42.** Which club did Cristiano Ronaldo join Manchester United from in 2003?

## ROUND 9: MOMENTS & MILESTONES
*13 questions - 1 point each unless stated*

**Q48.** Who scored the winning goal in the 2006 Champions League Final, as Barcelona came from behind to beat Arsenal 2-1?
> **Answer:** Juliano Belletti (Eto'o equalised, then Belletti scored the winner late on)

**Q49.** England beat Germany 5-1 in Munich in September 2001. Who scored a hat-trick in that match?
> **Answer:** Michael Owen (Gerrard and Heskey scored the other two)

**Q50.** Who is the youngest player to appear in a Premier League match?
> **Answer:** Ethan Nwaneri (Arsenal, aged 15 years and 181 days, vs Brentford in September 2022)

**Q51.** Which player scored four goals as a substitute in a single Premier League match?
> **Answer:** Ole Gunnar Solskjaer (Manchester United 8-1 Nottingham Forest, February 1999)

**Q52.** Who scored the fastest goal in Premier League history?
> **Answer:** Shane Long - 7.69 seconds (Southampton vs Watford, April 2019)

**Q53.** Who holds the record for the fastest red card in Premier League history? Bonus point for how many seconds.
> **Answer:** Keith Gillespie - 12 seconds (Sheffield United vs Reading, January 2007 — elbowed a player before the ball was even in play)

**Q54.** Who is the all-time top scorer in men's World Cup history?
> **Answer:** Miroslav Klose - 16 goals (Germany, across 2002, 2006, 2010 & 2014 World Cups). He overtook Ronaldo (Brazil, 15) in 2014.

**Q55.** In the 2008 Champions League Final between Manchester United and Chelsea, who missed the decisive penalty that handed United the trophy?
> **Answer:** Nicolas Anelka (saved by Edwin van der Sar). John Terry had earlier slipped and missed what would have been the winning penalty for Chelsea.

**Q56.** Who was the first player to score 100 Premier League goals?
> **Answer:** Alan Shearer

**Q57.** Who holds the record for the most goals scored in a single World Cup tournament?
> **Answer:** Just Fontaine - 13 goals (France, 1958 World Cup). A record that still stands.

**Q58.** In the famous Premier League match where Arsenal led Newcastle 4-0 before drawing 4-4 in 2011: (1 point each)

3. Which Arsenal winger scored twice in the first half?
> **Answer:**
> 1. Joey Barton
> 2. Cheick Tioté
> 3. Theo Walcott

**Q59.** When Wayne Rooney scored his famous overhead kick in the Manchester derby in 2011: (1 point each)
1. Which player crossed the ball?
2. What was the final score?
> **Answer:**
> 1. Nani
> 2. 2-1 (to Manchester United)

**Q60.** In the famous Gerrard slip match in 2014, when Liverpool lost to Chelsea and effectively handed the title to Manchester City — which Chelsea player scored after Gerrard slipped?
> **Answer:** Demba Ba

## ROUND 10: CAREERS & MANAGERS
*12 questions - 1 point each unless stated*

**Q61.** Who is the only player to have won the Champions League with three different clubs?
> **Answer:** Clarence Seedorf (Ajax 1995, Real Madrid 1998, AC Milan 2003 & 2007)

**Q62.** Who was the first African player to win the Ballon d'Or?
> **Answer:** George Weah (1995, while at AC Milan)

**Q63.** Which player scored the last ever goal at the old Wembley Stadium before it was demolished?
> **Answer:** Dietmar Hamann (Germany beat England 1-0 in a World Cup qualifier, October 2000 — the final match at the old Wembley)

**Q64.** Name the three Italian clubs that Zlatan Ibrahimovic played for. (1 point each)
> **Answer:** Juventus, Inter Milan, AC Milan

**Q65.** Zinedine Zidane nearly signed for which English club as a young player before joining Juventus in 1996?
> **Answer:** Blackburn Rovers (manager Ray Harford and owner Jack Walker reportedly turned down the chance to sign him)

**Q66.** Which Premier League manager was nicknamed "The Tinkerman" due to his constant rotation of players?
> **Answer:** Claudio Ranieri (at Chelsea, before being replaced by Jose Mourinho in 2004)

**Q67.** Only two players have scored a hat-trick in a World Cup Final. Name both. (1 point each)
> **Answer:** Geoff Hurst (England vs West Germany, 1966) and Kylian Mbappe (France vs Argentina, 2022). Mbappe scored his despite France losing on penalties.

**Q68.** Before establishing himself at Tottenham, Harry Kane went on four loan spells. Name any two of the four clubs. (1 point each)
> **Answer:** Leyton Orient, Millwall, Norwich City, Leicester City

**Q69.** Which was the first club to be deducted points in the Premier League, and why? (1 point each)
> **Answer:** Middlesbrough — deducted 3 points in 1996-97 for failing to fulfil a fixture against Blackburn Rovers (they called off the match claiming too many players were injured)

**Q70.** Who holds the record for the most consecutive Premier League appearances?
> **Answer:** Brad Friedel — 310 consecutive appearances (across Blackburn, Aston Villa, and Tottenham, from 2004 to 2012)

**Q71.** In Liverpool's famous 2005 Champions League Final comeback against AC Milan: (1 point each)
1. Who scored Liverpool's first goal of the comeback?
2. Which goalkeeper saved the decisive penalty in the shootout?
> **Answer:**
> 1. Steven Gerrard (headed goal to make it 3-1)
> 2. Jerzy Dudek

**Q72.** When Tottenham completed their comeback against Ajax in the 2019 Champions League semi-final second leg, who scored all three of Tottenham's goals?
> **Answer:** Lucas Moura (hat-trick, with the winner in the 96th minute)

*3 questions - 2 points each*

**Q73.** What connects the following: Blackburn Rovers (1995), Leicester City (2016), and Nottingham Forest (1978)?
> **Answer:** All won the top division of English football (League title / Premier League) having been promoted from the second tier the previous season or shortly before. *(Accept: surprise/shock title winners, or won the league shortly after promotion)*

**Q74.** What do Nicolas Anelka, Ashley Cole, William Gallas, Emmanuel Petit, and David Luiz all have in common?
> **Answer:** They all played for both Arsenal AND Chelsea.

**Q75.** What connects Sergio Aguero's goal vs QPR (2012), Steven Gerrard's goal vs AC Milan (2005 CL Final), and Ole Gunnar Solskjaer's goal vs Bayern Munich (1999 CL Final)?
> **Answer:** All were dramatic late goals that turned seemingly certain defeat into victory and won a major trophy.
